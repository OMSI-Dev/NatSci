# component wiring layout of breadboard build

PIR sensor pinout:
 	5V red wire > VOUT of Pololu
 	GND white wire > GND rail
 	DATA black wire > GPIO, 4.7k pull up resistor !!!!

Adafruit Pro tinker pinout:
 	BAT+ > power rail
 	G > GND rail
 	GPIO 6~ > Adafruit Pixie
 	BLK > Adafruit "6" BLK (ground connection)
 	A4 > Adafruit "6" A4

Adafruit Pixie LED pinout:
 	black wire > GND
 	white wire > GPIO 8~ (ylw)
 	red wire > power rail (blue)
 
Ground/power rail sequence:
[adafruit pro trinket]
[bulk capacitor]
[adafruit pixie]
[Pololu]

Pololu 5V Step-Up Voltage regulator:
 	V IN > power rail
 	GND > GND rail
 	V OUT > PIR sensor red wire

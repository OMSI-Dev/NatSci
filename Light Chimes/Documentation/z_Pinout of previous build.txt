# pinout layout of previous build

Pololu 5V Step-Up Voltage regulator:
 	V IN > power rail
 	GND > GND rail
 	V OUT > PIR sensor red wire

PIR sensor pinout:
 	red wire to VOUT of Pololu
 	white wire > GND rail
 	black wire > GPIO, 4.7k pull up resistor !!!!

Adafruit tinker pinout:
 	(right side)
 	BAT+ > power rail
 	G > ground rail
 	GPIO 6~ > white wire of external lamp
 	(left side)
 	BLK to BLK on adafruit "6"
 	A4 to A4 on adafruit "6"

External lamp pinout:
 	(from top view)
 	black wire > GND
 	white wire > GPIO 9~ (ylw)
 	red wire > power rail (blue)
 
Xbee wireless adapter pinout:
 	black wire > GND
 	blue wire (...)
 	green wire > power rail
 	yellow wire > TX
 	orange wire to GPIO 4
 	red wire (...)
 
Ground/power rails
[adafruit]
[bulk capacitor]
[external lamp]
[Pololu]

Note: there also was an Adafruit sound board for audio output
